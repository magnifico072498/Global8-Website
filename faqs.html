FAQs: For Your Reference

There are 2 ways to apply for a non-collateral business loan with us:
- Fill out the application form here and wait for a message or email.
- Apply in our office or through any of our loan specialists, and accredited agents nationwide.

Your business should be operating for a minimum of one (1) year, and you should have a checking account (CA) that has been active for at least six (6) months. See list of requirements here.

We only accept clients who have an existing checking account because we receive our client's loan payments through post-dated checks (PDCs).

Processing takes up to within seven (7) business days from the receipt of complete application documents.

You will receive an email, and a text message on the results of your application. A loan specialist or an accredited agent will contact you as soon as it has been approved!

Payment is done through submission of post-dated checks (PDCs) upon the release of the loan.

Global8 Finance Corp focuses to helping local businesses grow. Its business model and processes have not been developed to support personal loans as of the moment.

The interest rate is subject to the approval of the credit committee. The rate may vary depending on the loan amount, and period of term. Interested candidates are encouraged to apply and schedule a meeting with us to discuss this further.
